CRIME FIGHTER

Crime Fighters are normal men or women who’ve trained
for years to become physically and mentally prepared to
be heroes. They tend to have a couple of different areas of
expertise in addition to equipment or gadgets with which
they  pursue  their  goals.  Because  they  tend  to  not  be  as
physically powerful as other types of heroes, they always
look for other means to defeat their foes, such as through
intimidation, psychology, or trickery.
