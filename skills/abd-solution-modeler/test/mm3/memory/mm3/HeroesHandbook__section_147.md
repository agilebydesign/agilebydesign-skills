WARRIOR

Warriors  naturally  excel  in  battle. They  are  versatile  and
have many of the skills of Martial Artists and Weapon Mas-
ters  but  the  strength  and  power  to  fight  alongside  the
Paragons and Powerhouses. They often owe their greater-
than-human abilities to genetic or technological modifi-
cation or to an otherworldly heritage.
