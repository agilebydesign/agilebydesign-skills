## Concepts (candidate)

**Character** [foundational] — participates in **Check**, **Attack**; owns **Ability**, **Skill**, **Advantage**, **Power**
**Ability** — contributes to **Check**, **Resistance**; acquired via **PowerPoint**
**Skill** — contributes to **Check**; acquired via **PowerPoint**
**Advantage** — modifies **Check**, **Attack**, **Defense**; acquired via **PowerPoint**
**Power** — provides **Effect**; costs **PowerPoint**
**PowerPoint** — spent on **Ability**, **Skill**, **Advantage**, **Power**
**Check** — d20 + modifiers vs **DifficultyClass**
**DifficultyClass** — target for **Check**
**Resistance** — opposes **Effect**
**Degree** — measures success/failure of **Check**
**Attack** — applies **Effect** against target
**Defense** — opposes **Attack** (Dodge, Parry, Fortitude, Toughness, Will)
**Condition** — result of **Effect** (Dazed, Stunned, Hindered, etc.)
**ActionRound** — orders turns; **Character** takes actions
**Initiative** — orders turn sequence in **ActionRound**
**Effect** — Damage, Affliction, Weaken, Healing, etc.; resisted by **Resistance**
**Modifier** — Extra or Flaw on **Effect**
**Descriptor** — tags **Effect** (Fire, Cold, Magic, etc.)
**Array** — groups alternate **Effect**s
**Device** — **Equipment** with powers; may be Removable
**Equipment** — gear, weapons, vehicles
**Weapon** — **Equipment** that inflicts **Effect**
**Vehicle** — **Equipment** for movement
**Headquarters** — base with features

## Mechanisms (likely)

- **Check resolution** — d20 + modifiers vs DC; degrees of success/failure
- **Resistance** — target rolls defense vs effect rank
- **Power point economy** — traits cost points; **Character** spends **PowerPoint**
- **Condition track** — effect degrees map to conditions
- **Modifiers on effects** — Extras and Flaws alter **Effect** cost and behavior

## Actors (likely)

- **Gamemaster** — sets **DifficultyClass**, runs NPCs, adjudicates
- **Player** — controls **Character**
- **Hero** — player **Character**
- **Villain** — antagonist **Character**
